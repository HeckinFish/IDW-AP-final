Which cat of mine loves me the most?

Hello and welcome to the Kitty-Off.

I currently have 3 cats who I love very dearly, but I've come here to ask the question: how much do they love me? This competition is designed to evaluate each cats behavior towards me and deduct who loves me the most. The winner shall be decided by public vote, see below for more info on each contestant!
