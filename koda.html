Koda

"The Veteran"

Koda is currently my oldest cat, having gotten him as a kitten in the fall of 2018.
I got him as a rescue when he was orphaned after hurricane Florence (I lived in Wilmington NC at the time)
and he made cross-country journey to Bellingham with my family and I.

He is not as outwardly affectionate as my other two cats but he is still a very loving kitty!
He likes to hide places most of the time and does not enjoy the presence of Kinni (Kosmo is tolerable),
but I think he is just jealous that I give her attention now too instead of just him.
